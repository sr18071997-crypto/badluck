Core Features

Immediate Sound Alerts

Automatic sound alerts play immediately when items with 0-3 days expiry are detected. Alerts continue until acknowledged.

Visual Color Coding

Red (Shaking): 0-3 days - Immediate action required
Orange: 4-7 days - Plan for usage
Green: 8-10 days - Safe zone

Import & Export

Full data backup/restore capabilities. Import from CSV/JSON. Export to PDF, Excel, CSV. Never lose your data.

Keyboard Shortcuts

Ctrl + S - Save product
Ctrl + E - Export data
Ctrl + I - Import data
1-7 - Navigate sections

Best Practices & Tips

Use Barcodes

Add unique barcodes for faster product identification and inventory management.

Regular Backups

Export your data weekly. Store backups in different locations for safety.

Custom Alert Sounds

Upload a distinct sound that your team can easily recognize for urgent alerts.

Team Training

Train staff to check "Expiring Soon" daily and respond to urgent alerts immediately.
